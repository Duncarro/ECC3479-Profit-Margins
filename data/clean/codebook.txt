#DATA CODEBOOK
All data is derived from seasonally adjusted ABS measures.

MARGINS: 
GOP: A measure of Gross Operaing Profits as a profit margin. The profit margin is constructed via GOP divided by Sales. 

GOS: A measure of Gross Operaing Surplus as a profit margin. The profit margin is constructed via GOS divided by Gross Value Added (GVA). 


BUSINESS CYCLE MEASURES:
GDP: Quarterly estimates of GDP via the chain volume method.

THW: Quarterly measure of total hours worked from labour force surveys.
